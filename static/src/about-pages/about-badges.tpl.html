Impactstory badges

Badges help make impactstory more fun, but they also show and important advantage of altmetrics: multi-dimensionality. By mining activity across a variety of online platforms, we get a since of what kind of impacts research is having, not just "how much" on a single arbitrary scale. We've got three levels of badges: Gold, Silver, and Bronze.

Bronze badge
Bronze badges are relatively easy to get. They're for interesting and fun achievements, and for early progress on more substantive kinds of impacts.
Silver badge
Silver badges are less common, and reflect more impressive achievements. If you got a silver badge, you did something that makes you stand out from the crowd.
Gold badge
Gold badges are rarely awarded, and celebrate major achievements. A gold badge is worth bragging about--most Impactstory users don't any.
